## Daily Engagement Routine

### Morning (15 min)

1. Find 3-5 viral posts from similar creators
2. Like 20-30 comments on each
3. Reply to 2-3 with image/GIF (early engagement)

### Afternoon (10 min)

1. Check notifications
2. Like and reply to comments on own posts
3. Follow 10-20 new accounts in niche

### Evening (15 min)

## Growth Tactics

### 1. Early Reply on Viral Posts

- Turn on notifications for big creators
- Reply within **first 2 minutes** of their posts
- Use image or GIF (higher visibility)
- Your profile gets seen by their audience

### 2. Strategic Following

- Follow accounts in your niche
- Many will follow back
- Keep follow/unfollow ratio natural
- Don't mass unfollow

### 3. Collaboration

- Find similar-sized creators
- Mutual retweets
- Shoutout exchanges
- Increases reach to new audiences

### 4. Reply to Comments on Own Posts

- Engage with fans
- Build community
- Redirect to Fanvue when appropriate
