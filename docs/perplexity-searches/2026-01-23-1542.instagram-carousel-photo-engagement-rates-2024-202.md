## Carousel vs. Reels Performance

The performance distinction depends on your goal:

**Reach (Reels advantage):** Reels achieve **1.36 times more reach** than carousels and **2.25 times more reach** than single photos[1]. Reels are Instagram's primary discovery engine, reaching nearly 2 billion users monthly[4].

**Engagement (Carousels advantage):** Carousels consistently win on meaningful interactions. They generate significantly more comments, saves, and likes[3][5]. The average engagement rate for carousels is **1.92%** compared to **1.74%** for Reels[3]. Carousels also receive **114% more engagement** than single-image posts[3].

A key advantage for carousels is Instagram's "second chance" algorithm feature—if someone scrolls past without engaging, the carousel may reappear starting with slide two, creating multiple opportunities for engagement[4].

## Fashion Influencer Strategy

For fashion content, **use both formats strategically**[2][4]:

- **Reels** for audience growth and discovery—fashion video content performs well on the Explore page
- **Carousels** for deeper engagement with existing followers—ideal for showcasing multiple outfit angles, styling tips, or product details that encourage saves and discussion

Carousels are **twice as likely to be saved** as Reels[3], making them particularly valuable for fashion content where followers want to reference looks later.
